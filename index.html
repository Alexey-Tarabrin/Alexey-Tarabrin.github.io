Stacy The Muscle

Stacy The Muscle

“Stacy The Muscle” is a mini slasher-platformer in which you play as the manliest and most savage barbarian called Stacy. The main goal of the game is to climb on top of the endless tower. All that you have is your faithful sword, beautiful body and tons of enemies. So fill the tower up with cute monster bodies and climb on a mountain of their corpses as high as you can! But be careful, Stacy has an invincible muscular body but still can’t breath under the water.
